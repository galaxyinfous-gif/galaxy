Our Journey

From Passion to Partnership

Galaxy IT & Marketing was founded in 2006 with a simple belief: small businesses deserve access to the same powerful technology and marketing strategies as the big players — without the big price tag.

Over the past 19+ years, we've grown from a one-person operation into a full-service IT and marketing agency serving businesses across Worcester, MetroWest, and all of New England.

We've helped construction companies, cleaning services, painters, roofers, landscapers, and dozens of other home service businesses transform their operations with CRM systems, automated sales funnels, local SEO, and reliable tech support.

Today, we're proud to be the all-in-one growth partner for 100+ local businesses — combining technology, marketing, and dedicated support under one roof.
