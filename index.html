Frequently Asked Questions

Galaxy IT & Marketing — Common Questions

Quick, honest answers about pricing, timelines, and how we work with home-service businesses.

What services does Galaxy IT & Marketing provide?

Galaxy IT is a Worcester, MA digital agency for home-service businesses. We set up CRMs, run local SEO and Google Business Profile optimization, build marketing automation, manage social media, and develop fast websites. Founded in 2007, we currently support 7 active clients across Massachusetts and New England.

How much does CRM setup cost for a small business?

CRM setup with Galaxy IT typically ranges from $500 to $2,500 one-time, plus the Bee Pro Hub platform at $97/month. We start every project with a free 30-minute assessment so the package matches your exact pipeline, ad spend, and team size — no fixed package, no surprise fees.

How long until I see results from local SEO?

Most Galaxy IT clients see clear local SEO movement within 60 to 90 days, with many landing in Google's top 3 map pack within 4 months. Wolfs Siding went from 0–2 leads to 31 per month. Alfa Painting jumped from 5K to 56K monthly Google impressions over 12 months.

Do you only work with home-service businesses?

We specialize in home-service contractors — painting, siding, HVAC, roofing, cleaning, electrical, plumbing, landscaping, and construction — because we understand their lead flow and seasonal cycles. We currently serve 7 active home-service clients but take on adjacent local-service businesses (medical, legal, fitness) on a case-by-case basis.

What areas does Galaxy IT serve in New England?

Galaxy IT & Marketing is headquartered at 186 Main St, Marlborough, MA 01752 and serves home-service businesses across all six New England states: Massachusetts, Connecticut, Rhode Island, New Hampshire, Vermont, and Maine. Our local landing pages cover 1,200+ cities and towns from Boston to Burlington.

Is the 30-minute free assessment really free?

Yes — 100% free, no credit card, no contract. The 30-minute call covers a full digital presence audit, local search and Google Business Profile review, competitor scan, and a personalized 90-day growth plan. Schedule online or call info@galaxyinfo.us. Zero obligation to sign up afterward.
